How to Use the Alfalah App

Step 1: Download the Alfalah App from the Play Store and open it.
Step 2: Log in or sign up for an account.
Step 3: Nearby Masajid will automatically appear on your screen.
Step 4: Select a Masjid to view its location, directions, and prayer timings.
Step 5: You can also search for a specific Masjid using the search bar.
